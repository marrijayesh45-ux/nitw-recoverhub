Campus Guidelines

Learn how the NITW RecoverHub initiative works, the protocol for handling lost items, and official campus drop-off zones.

About the Initiative

Initiated by the Dean of Students Welfare Office, the NITW RecoverHub is designed to be the central repository for all lost and found items within the National Institute of Technology Warangal campus. Our goal is to foster a campus culture of integrity and ensure that valuable electronics, IDs, and personal belongings find their way back to their rightful owners as swiftly as possible.

Protocol for Lost & Found

If you LOST something:

1. Check the Search Registry immediately.
2. If not found, log a detailed Lost Report.
3. Provide distinct identifying marks in your description.
4. Check your institutional email regularly for updates.

If you FOUND something:

1. Do not leave the item unattended.
2. Log the item in the RecoverHub Registry.
3. If it contains sensitive data, turn it in to an official drop-off zone immediately.
4. Do not hand it over to unverified individuals.

Official Drop-Off Zones

If you prefer not to hold onto a found item, or if the item is of high value, please surrender it to any of the following authorized campus locations:

  • Student Activity Centre (SAC) Main Administrative Desk
  • Central Library Front Circulation & Security Desk
  • Main Campus Security Gate 1 Security Checkpoint
